Two Modes, Two Uses

Choose the right mode: GiftRoulette offers two distinct experiences for different situations. Temporary sessions for group events, permanent Duo Mode for stable couples.
Privacy guaranteed: All features comply with GDPR. Temporary sessions auto-delete after 24 hours, permanent Duo data can be deleted anytime.

Group Sessions TEMPORARY 24H

Ideal for: Christmas parties with friends, birthdays, corporate events, occasional gift exchanges between groups.

Key Features

24h Auto-Delete

Data automatically disappears 24 hours after last activity

Shared QR Code

Everyone scans the same code to join the session

Synchronized Draw

Simultaneous tap to reveal who you'll gift to

Totally Anonymous

No personal data required, only chosen names

How to Use Sessions

  • Create Session: The organizer clicks "Create Session", enters name and generates QR Code
  • Share QR: Show the QR Code to other participants in person or via screenshot
  • Participants: Others click "Join" and scan the QR Code
  • Waiting Room: Everyone sees the participant list in real-time
  • Draw: When ready, the organizer clicks "Draw" (everyone must be online)
  • Reveal: Synchronized animation, tap to reveal who you'll gift to
  • History: Result saved locally for 24h, then automatically deleted from Firebase
Important: All participants must be online during the draw for correct synchronization. After 24h cloud data is automatically deleted, but the result remains in your local history.

Duo Mode PERMANENT

Ideal for: Couples, best friends, close family members who want to share wishlists all year long.

Key Features

Secret Lists

Your wishlist is invisible to partner until draw

Random Draw

Partner draws a random gift from your list

Purchase Links

Add links to facilitate buying the drawn gift

Permanent

Data saved until you decide to manually delete it

Frequently Asked Questions

Group Sessions

Q: What happens if someone leaves during the draw?
A: The draw continues, but the result for who leaves might not sync. Better to complete the draw all together.

Q: Can I save the QR Code to reuse it?
A: Yes, but after 24h from last activity the session is deleted from Firebase. You'll need to create a new one.

Q: How can I see the draw history?
A: Click the clock icon on the home. History is saved locally on your device and doesn't auto-delete.

Q: Can I create multiple sessions simultaneously?
A: Yes, each session has a unique code. You can participate in multiple different group sessions.

Duo Mode

Q: Can my partner see my wishlist?
A: No! Your list remains completely hidden until your partner clicks "Draw Gift". At that point they see only 1 randomly drawn gift, not the entire list.

Q: Can I have multiple Duo Modes simultaneously?
A: No, you can be in only one Duo at a time. You must leave the current Duo to create/join a new one.

Q: What does my partner see when drawing?
A: The app randomly selects ONE gift from your list and shows it to both (name + optional link). Your partner doesn't see the other gifts in your list!

Q: Can I draw multiple times from the same list?
A: Yes, each draw is random. You might receive different gifts or the same gift multiple times. It's like a lottery!

Q: What happens if one leaves the Duo?
A: When one leaves, ALL data is permanently deleted for both. The other user will be notified and sent back to home.

Q: Are links mandatory?
A: No, you can add only the gift name. The link is optional to indicate where to buy it or show the specific product.

Q: Can I modify the list after drawing?
A: Yes! You can add/remove gifts anytime. If your partner draws again, they might get a different gift from the updated list.
